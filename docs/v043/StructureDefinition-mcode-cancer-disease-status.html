Resource Profile: Cancer Disease Status

Official URL: http://cocapacitacion.org/StructureDefinition/mcode-cancer-disease-status Version: 0.1.0
Draft as of 2024-09-04 Computable Name: CancerDiseaseStatus

A clinician's qualitative judgment on the current trend of the cancer, e.g., whether it is stable, worsening (progressing), or improving (responding).
